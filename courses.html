Courses I Teach

  • Introduction to Food Technology: This course covers the basics of food science, including nutrition, food safety, and culinary skills.
  • Advanced Culinary Arts: A hands-on course focused on advanced cooking techniques and the art of food presentation.
  • Nutrition and Wellness: This course explores the role of nutrition in health and wellness, with practical applications for everyday living.
  • Food Production and Management: Students learn about the food industry, including production, management, and sustainability practices.
