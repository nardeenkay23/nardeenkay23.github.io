About Me

Professional educator with diverse experience and strong track record fostering child-centred curriculum and student creativity. Adept at creating a classroom atmosphere that is stimulating, encouraging, and adaptive to the varied needs of students. Committed to professional ethics, standards of practice and the care and education of young children.I am a passionate educator with a background in Food Technology. Over the years, I have worked with diverse student groups, developing a teaching approach that is both inclusive and effective. Highly motivated, enthusiastic and dedicated secondary Food Technology teacher who wants all students to be successful learners. Proven abilities in problem solving, classroom management and motivation. Not only experienced in developing curriculum, but also in conducting teacher training, parenting programs and event management.

I hold a degree in Education with a specialization in Food Technology from Australian Catholic University, and I have over 10 years of experience in teaching at the secondary level.

My journey in education has been driven by a commitment to student success and a belief in the transformative power of learning.
